Rebecca N.1

  • Rebecca N. was born calculated 1871 in Illinois, United States.2
  • She and Edward G. Hall were married calculated 1889.3
  • Rebecca was enumerated on the 1910 Census census in the household of her spouse in Cordell, Washita County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated on East Street were:
    1. Hall, Edward G., head, age 45, works as a butcher for wages.
    2. -----, Rebecca, wife, age 40, given birth to 7 children, 6 are still living.
    3. -----, Ada, daughter, age 20, works as a printer for the newspaper.
    4. -----, Blanche, age 18.
    5. -----, Earl, age 17.
    6. -----, Floyd, age 11.
    7. -----, Frank, age 9.
    8. -----, Laura, age 3.4
  • In September 1918, Rebecca resided at Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States.5
  • She appeared on the 1920 census on 14 January 1920 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated at 803 11th Street were:
    1. Hall, Rebecca, head, age 49, married.
    2. -----, Blanche, daughter, age 26, works as a school teacher.
    3. -----, Earl, son, age 25, is a dry goods salesman.
    4. -----, Floyd, son, age 21, is a manager for a meat market.
    5. -----, Frank, son, age 19, is a salesman at a meat market.
    6. -----, Laura, daughter, age 13, attends school.
    7. Brodstreet, Blanche, boarder, age 23, is a school teacher.
    8. Means, Ethel, boarder, age 24, single, works as a school teacher.
    9. Huffins, Earl J., boarder, age 35, married, works as a manager at a grain company.
    10. -----, Annie, boarder, married, age 30.
    11. -----, Genevieve, boarder, age 2.6
