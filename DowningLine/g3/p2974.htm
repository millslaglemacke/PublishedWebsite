John Hamrick1

  • John Hamrick was born about 1834 in Arkansas, United States.1
  • He and Rhoda S. Downing were married on 8 February 1866.
  • He appeared on the 1870 census on 18 September 1870 in Precinct 4, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Hamrick, John, age 34, farmer.
    2. -----, Rhoda, age 34.
    3. -----, Rebecca, age 3.
    4. -----, James L., age 6 months.
    5. Denton, Alice, age 12.
    6. -----, Tipton, age 7.
    7. -----, Laura, age 6.
    The family was enumerated next to William and Charity Downing, Rhoda's parents. Also living next door are Mary and Florida Denton, Rhoda's two oldest children.1
