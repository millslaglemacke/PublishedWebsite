Jacob Genheimer

Parents

  • Father: Hans Steffa Genheimer (b. about 1678, d. about 1745)
  • Mother: Catarina Volker (b. after 1685)
  • Jacob Genheimer was born on 21 October 1714 in Oppau, Palatinate, Bavaria, Germany.
  • He immigrated on the ship Mary in 1732 to American Colonies.
  • He and Barbara Becker were married estimated 1735.
  • He died about 1755 in Fairfield County, South Carolina, American Colonies.
