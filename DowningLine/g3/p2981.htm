Obediah Downing1

Parents

  • Father: Thomas B. Downing (b. October 1841)
  • Mother: Susana Bur (b. March 1845)
  • Obediah Downing was also known as Abe Downing.2
  • He was born in July 1874 in Texas, United States.1,2
  • He appeared on the 1880 census in the household of his parents in Precinct 4, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, TB Downing, age 39, farmer.
    2. -----, Susana, age 36.
    3. -----, Thos F., age 19.
    4. -----, Wm, age 18.
    5. -----, Obediah, age 6.
    6. -----, Elbert B., age 3.
    7. Key, Emma, age 25, orphan.1
  • Obediah was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1900 Census in Justice Precinct #4, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, TB, head,age 58, works as a blacksmith, owns his house free.
    2. -----, Susan, wife, age 55, birthed 7 children - 5 still living.
    3. -----, Dora, daughter, age 19.
    4. -----, Abe (Obediah), son, age 25, works as a printer.
    5. -----, Albert (Elbert?), son, age 22, works as a farm laborer.
    6. -----, John, age 14, at school for 8 months.
    7. Beer, Rachel, MIL.2
