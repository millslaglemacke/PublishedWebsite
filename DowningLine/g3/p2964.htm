Donald Keith Kennemer

Parents

  • Father: Styles Martin Kennemer (b. 8 October 1892, d. 19 December 1984)
  • Mother: Gladys Shepherd (b. 21 January 1894, d. 9 September 1956)
  • Donald Keith Kennemer was born about July 1927 in Texas, United States.1
  • Donald was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Wichita Falls, Wichita County, Texas, United States. Enumerated at 2203 9th St were:
    1. Shepherd, Addie, head, age 61, no occupation.
    2. -----, Charles H. ab, son, age 21, single, attends school. [NOTE: I think the ab written after his name means he was absent. He is also marked for attending school so I think maybe he was living at the school.]

    4. -----, Gladis, daughter, age 36, no occupation.
