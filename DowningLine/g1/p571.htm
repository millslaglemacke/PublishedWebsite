Elisabeth Downing1

Parents

  • Father: William T. Downing, Junior (b. 4 September 1814, d. 29 January 1874)
  • Mother: Charity Brown (b. 9 January 1817, d. after 1880)
  • Elisabeth Downing was born about 1848 in Texas, United States.1
  • She appeared on the 1860 census in the household of her parents in Greenfield Post Office, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Living in this house were:
    1. Downing, William, age 45, works as a farmer, real estate valued at $2000, personal estate valued at $850.
    2. -----, Charity, age 44.
    3. Knight, Rebecca, age 21 [She is William & Charity's widowed daughter.]
    4. Downing, Thomas, age 18, works as farm labor.
    5. -----, Joel, age 16.
    6. -----, John, age 14.
    7. -----, Elisabeth, age 12, attends school.
    8. -----, Baalam, age 6, attends school.
    9. -----, Obediah, age 3.
    10. Knight, William, age 2, born TX. [He is Rebecca's son and William and Charity's grandson.].
    Living in the next house enumerated is William's brother, Carter L. Downing. Missing from the household are William and Mary. I suspect that William and Joel are the same person based on their birthdates and the fact that neither one appear on the census records together.1
