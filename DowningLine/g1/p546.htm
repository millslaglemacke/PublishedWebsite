Travis Bell Downing1

Parents

  • Father: Baalam Downing (b. 6 January 1854, d. 19 March 1916)
  • Mother: Susan Mills (b. estimated 1854, d. before 1880)
  • Travis Bell Downing was born on 4 October 1879 in Greenville, Hunt County, Texas, United States.1,2,3,4
  • Travis was listed as a nephew on the 1880 census of Obediah Carter Downing enumerated on 24 June 1880 in District #2, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, OC, age 24, farmer.
    2. -----, AO, age 20.
    3. -----, Allie, age 1, born TX.
    4. -----, BM, age 26 (relationship to head - brother), farmer.
    5.-----, Travis B, age 8/12 (relationship to head - newphew).5
  • He appeared on the 1900 census on 12 June 1900 in Justice Precinct #1, Hunt County, Texas, United States. He was living in the household of H. Ruckers as a border. Below is his census entry:
    Travis Downing, border, born Oct 1877, single, born TX, parents born TX, farm laborer, can read, write, and speak English.6
  • Travis Bell Downing and Minnie E. Pillers were married on 6 July 1902 in Wise County, Texas, United States.7
  • He appeared on the 1910 census on 27 April 1910 in Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in the 3rd Justice Precinct were:
    1. Downing, Travis, age 36, farmer, schedule 121
    2. ------, Minnie, age 27.
    3. -----, Louis, age 6, attends school.
    4. -----, Gilbert, age 5.
    5. -----, Faybell, age 3.1
  • Travis registered for the draft on 12 September 1918 in Dalhart, Dallam County, Texas, United States. He was working as a car carpenter in Dallam. He was described as having medium height, medium build, blue eyes, and brown hair.8
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 4 April 1930 in Justice District #8, Donley County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household are:
    1. Downing, Travis B., age 50.
    2. -----, Minnie, age 47.
    3. -----, Allice, age 12, attends school.
    4. -----, Jack, age 8, attends school.
    5. -----, Bill, age 6.9
  • He died on 4 March 1953, at age 73, in Bowie Hospital, Bowie, Texas, in Bowie, Montague County, Texas County, United States.10
  • He was buried on 6 March 1953 in Alvord Cemetery, Alvord, TX, in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States.10
