Molly Verona Kennemer Downing
  • Molly Verona Kennemer was born on 18 June 1887 in Texas, United States.1,2
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Justice Precinct #5, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Kennemer, GW, age 39, rents farm.
    2. -----, Lee H., age 33, given birth to 8 children, 7 children living.
    3. -----, Daisy W(m?), age 15.
    4. -----, Mollie V., age 12.
    5. -----, Victor R., age 10.
    6. -----, Styles M., age 7.
    7. -----, William G., age 5.
    8. -----, Minnie B., age 3.
    9. -----, Max, age 0/12.2
  • She and Thomas ("Oscar") Downing were married on 9 July 1905 in Wise County, Texas, United States.3
  • Molly was enumerated on the 1910 census in the household of her spouse in Justice Precinct #3, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household:
    1. Downing, Oscar, age 28, works as a farmer, able to read and write, owns the farm free.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 21, one child born and living.
    3. -----, Olive, age 2.4
  • Molly was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Thomas O., rented home, age 38, worked a a laborer in oil refining for a wage.
    2. -----, Mollie B., age 32.
    3. -----, Gertrude, age 12, attended school.
    4. -----, Nell, age 4 years 8 months.5
  • Molly was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Justice Precinct #2, Wichita County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Oscar; renting at $10 monthly, owns radio, age 48, worked in oil fields, not a veteran.
    2. -----, Mollie; age 42.
    3. -----, Nellie; age 14, attending school.
    4. -----, Billie J.; age 8, attending school.6
  • Molly was living with her spouse on 1 April 1935 in Union County, New Mexico, United States.7
  • Molly was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Montague County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. -----, Oscar, age 58, he worked as a farmer for 50 weeks on his own account, he received more than $50 doing other work.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 52. .
    3. -----, Nell, age 23, marital status is divorced
    4. -----, Billie Jane, age 18, attending high school.7
  • Molly was living with her spouse in 1942 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States.8
  • Between 1972 and 1974, Molly resided in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.9
  • She died on 19 February 1974, at age 86, in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.9
  • She was buried on 21 February 1974 in Oaklawn Cemetery, Decatur, Wise County, Texas, United States.9,10
