Tipton Denton1

  • Tipton Denton was born about 1832 in Missouri, United States.1
  • He and Rhoda S. Downing were married about 1853.1
  • He appeared on the 1860 census on 14 September 1860 in Black Jack Grove Post Office, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Denton, Tipton, age 28, real estate valued at $840, personal estate valued at $1,725.
    2. -----, Rhoda S., age 23.
    3. -----, Mary B., age 6.
    4. -----, Florida C., age 4.
    5. -----, Alice E., age 2.1
  • He died between 1864 and 1866 in Hunt County, Texas, United States.
