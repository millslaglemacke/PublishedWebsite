Thomas ("Oscar") Downing1

Parents

  • Father: Baalam Downing (b. 6 January 1854, d. 19 March 1916)
  • Mother: Sarah Jane Lee (b. January 1854, d. 16 February 1941)
  • Thomas ("Oscar") Downing was born on 27 November 1881 in Greenfield, Hunt County, Texas, United States.2,3,4,5,6,7,8,1,9
  • He appeared on the 1900 census in the household of his parents in Justice Precinct #1, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, B.M., born Jan 1854, age 46, farmer, can read and write, owns farm free..
    2. -----, Sarah J., born Jan 1854, age 46, has had 2 children, both still living.
    3.-----, Mattie, born Apr 1878, age 22, (She is listed as B.M.'s daughter and not step-daughter)
    4. -----, Oscar, born Nov 1881, age 18.2
  • He and Molly Verona Kennemer were married on 9 July 1905 in Wise County, Texas, United States.10
  • He appeared on the 1910 census on 26 April 1910 in Justice Precinct #3, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household:
    1. Downing, Oscar, age 28, works as a farmer, able to read and write, owns the farm free.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 21, one child born and living.
    3. -----, Olive, age 2.11
  • Thomas registered for the draft on 12 September 1918 in Petrolia, Clay County, Texas, United States. He was employed as a laborer for the Texas Company in Petrolia, He was described as having medium height, a medium build, blue eyes, and dark hair.7
  • He appeared on the 1920 census on 6 January 1920 in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Thomas O., rented home, age 38, worked a a laborer in oil refining for a wage.
    2. -----, Mollie B., age 32.
    3. -----, Gertrude, age 12, attended school.
    4. -----, Nell, age 4 years 8 months.12
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 11 April 1930 in Justice Precinct #2, Wichita County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Oscar; renting at $10 monthly, owns radio, age 48, worked in oil fields, not a veteran.
    2. -----, Mollie; age 42.
    3. -----, Nellie; age 14, attending school.
    4. -----, Billie J.; age 8, attending school.13
  • On 1 April 1935, Thomas resided in Union County, New Mexico, United States.14
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 18 April 1940 in Montague County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. -----, Oscar, age 58, he worked as a farmer for 50 weeks on his own account, he received more than $50 doing other work.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 52. .
    3. -----, Nell, age 23, marital status is divorced
    4. -----, Billie Jane, age 18, attending high school.14
  • In 1942, Thomas resided in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States.15
  • Thomas registered for the draft on 27 April 1942 in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States. He was 60 years old and self employed.8
  • He died on 11 February 1949, at age 67, in Decatur, Wise County, Texas, United States.1,16
  • He was buried on 13 February 1949 in Oaklawn Cemetery, Decatur, Wise County, Texas, United States.17,18,19
