Olive ("Gertrude") Downing1

Parents

  • Father: Thomas ("Oscar") Downing (b. 27 November 1881, d. 11 February 1949)

Gertrude "Diddle" Downing Hall
  • Olive ("Gertrude") Downing was born on 22 June 1907 in Texas, United States.2,3,4
  • Olive was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1910 Census in Justice Precinct #3, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household:
    1. Downing, Oscar, age 28, works as a farmer, able to read and write, owns the farm free.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 21, one child born and living.
    3. -----, Olive, age 2.5
  • Olive was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1920 Census in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Thomas O., rented home, age 38, worked a a laborer in oil refining for a wage.
    2. -----, Mollie B., age 32.
    3. -----, Gertrude, age 12, attended school.
    4. -----, Nell, age 4 years 8 months.6
  • She and Frank Van Hall were married on 6 December 1925 in Henrietta, Clay County, Texas, United States.7,8
  • Olive was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Living at 613 South 13th were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 29, works as a meat cutter in a meat marker for wages.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 22.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 2 years 7 months.7
  • Olive was living with her spouse between January 1934 and January 1936 at Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States.9,10,11
  • Olive was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated at 503 N. Main were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 39, he worked 63 hours the previous week as partner proprietor of a retail grocery store.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 32.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 12, has completed 6 years of school.
    4. -----, Joe F., age 6, has completed 0 years of school.12
  • Before 2000, Olive resided in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.3
  • She died on 22 February 2000, at age 92, in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.13,3,14
  • She was buried estimated 25 February 2000 in Mount Vernon Cemetery, Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.14
