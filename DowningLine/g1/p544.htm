Frank Van Hall
  • Frank Van Hall was born on 11 September 1900 in Oklahoma, United States.2,3,4,5,1,6
  • He appeared on the 1910 Census census in the household of his parents in Cordell, Washita County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated on East Street were:
    1. Hall, Edward G., head, age 45, works as a butcher for wages.
    2. -----, Rebecca, wife, age 40, given birth to 7 children, 6 are still living.
    3. -----, Ada, daughter, age 20, works as a printer for the newspaper.
    4. -----, Blanche, age 18.
    5. -----, Earl, age 17.
    6. -----, Floyd, age 11.
    7. -----, Frank, age 9.
    8. -----, Laura, age 3.7
  • Frank was living in the household of his mother at Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States, in September 1918.8
  • Frank registered for the draft on 12 September 1918 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. He was 18 years old and a student at Frederick High School when he registered. He was described as having medium height, a medium build, brown eyes, dark hair and not obviously physically disqualified.8
  • He appeared on the 1920 census in the household of his mother in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated at 803 11th Street were:
    1. Hall, Rebecca, head, age 49, married.
    2. -----, Blanche, daughter, age 26, works as a school teacher.
    3. -----, Earl, son, age 25, is a dry goods salesman.
    4. -----, Floyd, son, age 21, is a manager for a meat market.
    5. -----, Frank, son, age 19, is a salesman at a meat market.
    6. -----, Laura, daughter, age 13, attends school.
    7. Brodstreet, Blanche, boarder, age 23, is a school teacher.
    8. Means, Ethel, boarder, age 24, single, works as a school teacher.
    9. Huffins, Earl J., boarder, age 35, married, works as a manager at a grain company.
    10. -----, Annie, boarder, married, age 30.
    11. -----, Genevieve, boarder, age 2.9
  • He and Olive ("Gertrude") Downing were married on 6 December 1925 in Henrietta, Clay County, Texas, United States.1,10
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 11 April 1930 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Living at 613 South 13th were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 29, works as a meat cutter in a meat marker for wages.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 22.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 2 years 7 months.1
  • Between January 1934 and January 1936, Frank resided at Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States.11,12,13
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 3 April 1940 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated at 503 N. Main were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 39, he worked 63 hours the previous week as partner proprietor of a retail grocery store.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 32.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 12, has completed 6 years of school.
    4. -----, Joe F., age 6, has completed 0 years of school.14
  • He died on 18 September 1994, at age 94, in Franklin County, Texas, United States.15,16
  • He was buried estimated 21 September 1994 in Mount Vernon Cemetery, Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.16
