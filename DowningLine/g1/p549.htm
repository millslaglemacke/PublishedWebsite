Martha "Mattie" Rebecca ("Mattie") Smith1,2

Parents

  • Father: William Champion Smith (b. 1842, d. after 15 June 1880)
  • Mother: Sarah Jane Lee (b. January 1854, d. 16 February 1941)
Mattie Smith Wariner
  • Martha "Mattie" Rebecca ("Mattie") Smith was also known as Matty R. Smith.1
  • Note.3
  • She was born on 17 April 1877 in Kerrville, Kerr County, Texas, United States.1,4,5,6,7,3
  • She appeared on the 1880 census in the household of her parents in Caldwell County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this dwelling were:
    1. Sawier, J. A. J, white, 23, married, farming.
    2. -----, Marthy, white, 19, wife, married.
    3. Farmer, Mary, black, female, 35, married, cooking.
    4. McMakoon, W. J., white, male, 30, hirling, single, farming.
    5. Smith, WC, white, male, 40, married, farming, unemployed 11 months, consumption.
    6. -----, Jane, white, female, 26, wife.
    7. -----, Matty R., white, female, 2, daughter.1
  • Martha resided with her mother and stepfather, Baalam Downing, on 14 June 1900 in Justice Precinct #1, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, B.M., born Jan 1854, age 46, farmer, can read and write, owns farm free..
    2. -----, Sarah J., born Jan 1854, age 46, has had 2 children, both still living.
    3.-----, Mattie, born Apr 1878, age 22, (She is listed as B.M.'s daughter and not step-daughter)
    4. -----, Oscar, born Nov 1881, age 18.4
  • She and George Winfield Wariner were married on 24 December 1901 in Wise County, Texas, United States, It appears they later separated or divorced. They are living separately on the 1940 census where George is listed as divorced and Mattie is listed as widowed. When George died in 1952 his maritial status was given as widowed but Mattie did not pass until 1961.5,8
  • Martha was enumerated on the 1910 census in the household of her spouse in 2nd Judicial Precinct, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wossener, George Wossener, head, white, age 31, dentist, own shop, owns his own house free.
    2. -----, Martha, wife, age 31, has given birth to 3 children - all of them are living.
    3. -----, Ethel, age 7.
    4. -----, Berne, age 5
    5. -----, Ila, age 2.
    6. Downing, Balam Downing, age 53, works as a County Commissioner for a wage. [He is George's father-in-law]
    7. -----, Sarah J., age 54. [She is George's mother-in-law].5
  • Martha was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. George Wariner, age 41, born Missouri, parents born US, works as a pipe fitter.
    2. -----, Mattie, age 38, born TX, parents born TX.
    3. -----, Verne, son, age 18 (15?).
    4. -----, Ila Myrle, daughter, age 11.
    5. -----, BM, son, age 7.
    6. Downing, Sarah Jane, mother-in-law, 66, widow, born IL, father born IL, mother born VA.6
  • She appeared on the 1940 census on 5 April 1940 in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wariner, Mattie, rents house for $6, age 60, widow.
    2. Downing, Sarah, age 86, widow, unable to work.9
  • In September 1961, Martha resided at Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.3
  • She died on 20 September 1961, at age 84, in Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.3
  • She was buried on 22 September 1961 in Greenwood Memorial Park, Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.3
