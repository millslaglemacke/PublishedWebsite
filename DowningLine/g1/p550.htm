George Wariner
  • George Winfield Wariner was born on 21 August 1878 in Missouri, United States.1,2
  • He has conflicting birth information of 31 August 1878 in Missouri, United States.3
  • He and Martha "Mattie" Rebecca ("Mattie") Smith were married on 24 December 1901 in Wise County, Texas, United States, It appears they later separated or divorced. They are living separately on the 1940 census where George is listed as divorced and Mattie is listed as widowed. When George died in 1952 his maritial status was given as widowed but Mattie did not pass until 1961.1,4
  • He appeared on the 1910 census on 10 April 1910 in 2nd Judicial Precinct, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wossener, George Wossener, head, white, age 31, dentist, own shop, owns his own house free.
    2. -----, Martha, wife, age 31, has given birth to 3 children - all of them are living.
    3. -----, Ethel, age 7.
    4. -----, Berne, age 5
    5. -----, Ila, age 2.
    6. Downing, Balam Downing, age 53, works as a County Commissioner for a wage. [He is George's father-in-law]
    7. -----, Sarah J., age 54. [She is George's mother-in-law].1
  • He appeared on the 1920 census on 27 January 1920 in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. George Wariner, age 41, born Missouri, parents born US, works as a pipe fitter.
    2. -----, Mattie, age 38, born TX, parents born TX.
    3. -----, Verne, son, age 18 (15?).
    4. -----, Ila Myrle, daughter, age 11.
    5. -----, BM, son, age 7.
    6. Downing, Sarah Jane, mother-in-law, 66, widow, born IL, father born IL, mother born VA.5
  • He appeared on the 1940 census as an in-law to John Barnett in Wheeler County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Bennett, John, head, age 50, worked 48 hours the previous week farming, worked for 52 weeks.
    2. -----, Mary F., wife, age 48,
    3. -----, Virginia W., daughter, age 18, attends school.
    4. -----, Genevieve, daughter, age 16, attends school.
    5. -----, Johnnie, son, age 13, attends school.
    6. -----, Keith H., son, age 9, attends school.
    7. -----, Jerry L., son, age 4, doesn't attend school.
    8. Wariner, George W., brother-in-law, age 63, divorced, worked 48 hours the previous week as a salesman.6
  • George Winfield Wariner died on 9 June 1952, at age 73, in Wichita Falls, Wichita County, Texas, United States.3,7,8,9
  • He was buried estimated 11 June 1952 in Memorial Gardens Cemetery, Wellington, Collingsworth County, Texas, United States.9,3
