Alexander Johnson1

Parents

  • Father: Archibald Johnson (b. 1761, d. 7 May 1822)
  • Mother: Julia Elizabeth Gordon (b. 1780, d. 1860)
  • Alexander Johnson was born on 2 August 1816 in Georgia, United States.1,2
  • He and Louise Amelia Tate Mann were married on 31 March 1846 in Dekalb, Grant County, Arkansas, United States.3
  • He and Mary Isabel Liddell were married on 15 November 1857 in Dekalb County, Georgia, United States.4
  • He died on 10 April 1888, at age 71, in Biardstown, Lamar County, Texas, United States.
