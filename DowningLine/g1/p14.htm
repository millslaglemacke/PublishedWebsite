Edward Louis Johann Jahn

Edward Louis Jahn, Sr.
  • Edward Louis Johann Jahn was born on 11 March 1915 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4
  • He served in the military as a Midshipman, third class while at the U.S. Naval Academy. on 9 June 1932 in Anapolis, Anne Arundel County, Maryland, United States.5
  • He served in the military the military between June 1933 and April 1948.6,2
  • In 1936, Edward resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. He is living with his parents and his occupation is "student."
  • In 1942, Edward resided in Camp Hood, Bell County, Texas, United States.7
  • He and Billie Jane Downing were married on 6 December 1942 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States, This couple planned to renew their vows on their 50th wedding anniversay but Billie Jane was ill around that time so they renewed their vows after 51 years. They filed for divorce in Oct 1993 so they could get remarried on Dec 6 1993.7,8,9
  • He was living at 4122 Antelope Trail before 1998. He lived there until his death.10
  • In November 1998, Edward resided in Temple, Bell County, Texas, United States.11
  • He died on 9 November 1998, at age 83, in Temple, Bell County, Texas, United States.12,11
