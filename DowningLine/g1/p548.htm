William Champion Smith1

  • Note.
  • William Champion Smith was born in 1842 in Tennessee, United States.1
  • He and Sarah Jane Lee were married in 1877 in Elizabethtown, Hardin County, Missouri, United States.2,1
  • He appeared on the 1880 census on 14 June 1880 in Caldwell County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this dwelling were:
    1. Sawier, J. A. J, white, 23, married, farming.
    2. -----, Marthy, white, 19, wife, married.
    3. Farmer, Mary, black, female, 35, married, cooking.
    4. McMakoon, W. J., white, male, 30, hirling, single, farming.
    5. Smith, WC, white, male, 40, married, farming, unemployed 11 months, consumption.
    6. -----, Jane, white, female, 26, wife.
    7. -----, Matty R., white, female, 2, daughter.1
  • He died after 15 June 1880 in Kirville, Texas, United States.

Family: Sarah Jane Lee (b. January 1854, d. 16 February 1941)
