Mary Ellen Skidmore1,2

Parents

  • Father: J. D. Skidmore (b. estimated 1870)
  • Mother: Martha Nations (b. estimated 1870)
  • Mary Ellen Skidmore was born on 21 September 1894 in Hopkins County, Texas, United States.1,3,4
  • She and Grover Cannaday were married about 1914 Grover was 21 and Mary was 19 when they married.1,3
  • Mary was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Precinct 1, Franklin County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Cannaday, Grover, age 37, works as a general farmer on his own account.
    2. -----, Mary E., wife, age 35.
    3. -----, Grover L., son, age 13, attends school.
    4. -----, Charles E., son, age 10.
    5. -----, J. C., son, age 3 years and 2 months.3
  • Mary moved to Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States, about 1954.4
  • About 1974, Mary resided in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.1
  • She died on 12 May 1974, at age 79, in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.1,4
  • She was buried on 13 May 1974 in Purley Cemetery, Purley, Franklin County, Texas, United States.4
