Elizabeth Johnston

  • Elizabeth Johnston was born on 17 April 1764 in Pennsylvania, American Colonies.
  • She and Moses Liddell, Senior, were married on 16 September 1784 in Abbeville, 96th District, South Carolina, United States.
  • She died on 4 November 1853, at age 89, in Gwinnett County, Georgia, United States.
