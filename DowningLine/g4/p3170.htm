Grover Cannaday1

  • Grover Cannaday was born about 1893 in Texas, United States.1
  • He and Mary Ellen Skidmore were married about 1914 Grover was 21 and Mary was 19 when they married.2,1
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 5 April 1930 in Precinct 1, Franklin County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Cannaday, Grover, age 37, works as a general farmer on his own account.
    2. -----, Mary E., wife, age 35.
    3. -----, Grover L., son, age 13, attends school.
    4. -----, Charles E., son, age 10.
    5. -----, J. C., son, age 3 years and 2 months.1
  • He died before 1974.3
