Cecil Jackson Austin1

Parents

  • Father: Joseph M. Austin (b. 16 April 1895)
  • Mother: Beulah Jones (b. about 1898)
  • Cecil Jackson Austin was born on 18 May 1923 in Horatio, Sevier County, Arkansas, United States.1,2
  • In 1943, Cecil resided in Shelby County, Tennessee, United States.2
  • He served in the military the Army during World War II. on 5 March 1943 in Fort Oglethorpe, Catoosa County, Georgia, United States.2
  • He and Dorothy Mae Baltzer were married on 26 January 1955 in Globe, Gila County, Arizona, United States.1
  • He died on 30 October 1971, at age 48, in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.1
  • He was buried estimated 2 November 1971 in Fort Whipple National Cemetery, Prescott, AZ, in Prescott, Yavapai County, Arizona, United States.1
