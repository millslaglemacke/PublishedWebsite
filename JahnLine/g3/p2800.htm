Donald Clyde Marlenee1

Parents

  • Father: David Clarence Marlenee (b. 9 April 1875)
  • Mother: Elma ("Jane") Keltner (b. 8 September 1873)1
  • Donald Clyde Marlenee was born on 22 December 1914 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • Donald was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3215 Grover were:
    1. Marlenee, David, owns his house valued at $1000, doesn't own a radio, age 52, works as a mail clerk in the insurance industry.
    2. -----, Elma, age 54.
    3. -----, Donald, age 15, attends school.2
  • He worked as a a typesetter for the newspaper after 1930.1
  • He and Rose Frances Sadil were married about 1935 in Papillion, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States, They later divorced.1
  • He died in November 1984, at age 69, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • He was buried estimated December 1984 in Westlawn-HIllcrest Memorial Park, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
