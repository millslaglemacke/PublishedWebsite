Jack Hall
  • Note.1
  • Jack Roger Hall was born on 17 September 1927 in Oklahoma, United States.1,2
  • Jack was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Living at 613 South 13th were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 29, works as a meat cutter in a meat marker for wages.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 22.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 2 years 7 months.2
  • Jack was living in the household of his parents at Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States, between January 1934 and January 1936.3,4,5
  • He appeared on the 1940 census in the household of his parents in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States. Enumerated at 503 N. Main were:
    1. Hall, Frank V., age 39, he worked 63 hours the previous week as partner proprietor of a retail grocery store.
    2. -----, Gertrude O., age 32.
    3. -----, Jack R., age 12, has completed 6 years of school.
    4. -----, Joe F., age 6, has completed 0 years of school.6
  • He and Blanche were married estimated 1950.
  • In 1966, Jack resided at Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States. He had been living in Fort Worth for 12 years.1
  • He died on 25 November 1966, at age 39, in All Saints Hospital, Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.1
  • He was buried on 28 November 1966 in Rose Hill Cemetery, Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.1
