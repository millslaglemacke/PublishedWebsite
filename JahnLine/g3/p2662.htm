Helen Ann Bilek1

Parents

  • Father: Frank John Bilek (b. 26 November 1900, d. 19 April 1988)
  • Mother: Helen Grace Freeburn (b. 27 May 1901, d. February 1976)
  • Helen Ann Bilek was born on 5 February 1925 in Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa, United States.2,3,1
  • Helen was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1930 Census in Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa, United States. Enumerated at 4315 Garretson Ave were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, rents his dwelling for $35, age 29, first married at 22, works as a draftsman in a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Helen, age 28.
    3. -----, Helen A., age 5 years, doesn't attend school.
    4. -----, Paula E., age 3 years 8 months.
    5. -----, Frances, age 2 years.2
  • Helen was living in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States, in 1935.3
  • She appeared on the 1940 census in the household of her parents in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated at 7556 South Winchester Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, rents his house for $45, age 39, worked for 50 hours the previous week as a draft engineer at a packing house machine mfg, he worked for 52 weeks in 1939 and earned $2800.
    2. -----, Helen, age 38.
    3. -----, Helen A., age 15, attends school.
    4. -----, Paula E., age 13, attends school.
    5. -----, Frances, age 12, attends school.
    6. -----, Thomas, age 4, doesn't attend school.3
  • She and Max Strain were married on 3 January 1943 in Elk Point, Union County, South Dakota, United States.4
  • She worked as a grocery store owner estimated 1960.4
