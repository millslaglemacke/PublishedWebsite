Matt Michael1

  • Matt Michael was born about 1866 in Germany.1
  • He immigrated from Germany in 1873 to United States. He was a naturalized citizen at the time of the 1930 census.1
  • He and Augusta Miller were married about 1900.1
  • He worked as a car inspector at a meat packing plant in 1930.1
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 2 April 1930 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1517 Berry Ave were:
    1. Michael, Matt, owns his residence which was valued at $3000; age 64, works as a car inspector at a meat packing plant.
    2. -----, Augusta, age 62.
    3. -----, Walter, age 28, was an unemployed laborer in a meat packing plant.
    4. -----, Emil, age 26, worked as a clerk in a meat packing plant.
    5. -----, Frederick, age 23, worked as a machinist at a typewriter company.
    6. -----, Ida, age 18, doesn't attend school, no occupation.1,2
