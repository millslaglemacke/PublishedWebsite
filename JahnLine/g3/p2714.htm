Anton Budin1

  • Anton Budin was born about 1873 in Bohemia.2,3
  • He immigrated in 1886 to United States. He became a naturalized citizen in 1906.4
  • He and Mary Korinek were married about 1896.1,3
  • He appeared on the 1910 census on 27 April 1910 in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Budin, Anton, head, age 37, works as a laborer in a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Mary, wife, age 33, given birth to 3 children, all are still living.
    3.-----, Rudolph J., son, age 12, attends school.
    4. -----, Joseph, son, age 10, attends school.
    5. -----, Mary A., daughter, age 6, attends school.
    6. Kosuirk, James, brother in law, age 16, works as a laborer in a packing house for wages.3
  • He appeared on the 1920 census on 12 January 1920 in Wood River, Dawson County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Budin, Anton, head, age 46, works as a general farmer on his own account.
    2. -----, Mary, wife, age 43.
    3. -----, Rudolph J., son, age 21, works as a farmer for wages.
    4. -----, Joseph, son, age 19, is able to speak English, works as a farmer for wages.
    5. -----, Mary A., daughter, age 15, hasn't attended school this year.
    6. Lukes, Albert, border, age 2 10/12.4
