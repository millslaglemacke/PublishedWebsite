Edna Wise1

  • Edna Wise was born about 1893 in Indiana, United States.2
  • She and Travers Othelia Barber were married before 1913.1
  • Edna moved with her spouse between April 1916 and September 1917 to Montana, United States.2
  • Edna was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in School District 111, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated at this house were:
    1. Barber, Travers O., head, rents dwelling, works on his own account on a general farm.
    2. -----, Edna, wife, age 26.
    3. -----, Lionel E., age 6, doesn't attend school.
    4. -----, John, age 3 8/12.
    5. -----, Robert T., age 2 3/12.
    6. -----, Gerald W., age 8/12.2
