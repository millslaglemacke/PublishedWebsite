Martha J. Black1

Parents

  • Father: William Black (b. 8 April 1811, d. 18 May 1859)
  • Mother: Nancy Perdy (b. 22 October 1814, d. 21 March 1864)
  • Martha J. Black was born on 29 September 1834 in Shawneetown, Gallatin County, Illinois, United States.2,3,4,5
  • She has conflicting birth information of 29 September 1834 in Kentucky, United States.3
  • She appeared on the 1850 census in the household of her parents in Elizabethtown, Hardin County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Black, William, age 39, male, blacksmith.
    2. -----, Nancy, age 29, female.
    3. Blevins, Joseph, age 25, male, blacksmith.
    4. Black, Sarah Ann, age 17, female.
    5. -----, Martha J., age 15, female.
    6. -----, Rosamid, age 13, female, attends school.
    7. -----, John, age 11, male, attends school
    8. -----, Isaac, age 9, male, attends school.
    9. -----, Mary E., age 5, female.
    10. -----, William H., age 1, male.6
  • She and Joseph N. Lee were married about 1851 in Illinois, United States.1
  • Martha was enumerated on the 1860 census in the household of her spouse in Cave In Rock Post Office, Hardin County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Lee, Joseph N., age 31, farmer, $2500 real estate, $600 personal estate.
    2. -----. Martha J., age 25, housekeeper.
    3. -----, Wm N., age 8.
    4. -----, Sara J., age 6.
    5. -----. John V., age 5.
    6. Black, Nancy, age 40, mother.
    7. -----, John, age 20.
    8. -----, Isaac, age 17.
    9. -----, Mary, age 13.
    10. -----, Eliza P., age 10.4
  • She and Alexander Ragan were married before 1868.2
  • She died on 19 August 1919, at age 84, in Robinson, Crawford County, Illinois, United States.2
