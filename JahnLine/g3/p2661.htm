Helen Grace Freeburn1

  • Helen Grace Freeburn was born on 27 May 1901 in Michigan, United States.1,2,3
  • She and Frank John Bilek were married about 1922.1,2
  • Helen was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa, United States. Enumerated at 4315 Garretson Ave were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, rents his dwelling for $35, age 29, first married at 22, works as a draftsman in a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Helen, age 28.
    3. -----, Helen A., age 5 years, doesn't attend school.
    4. -----, Paula E., age 3 years 8 months.
    5. -----, Frances, age 2 years.2
  • Helen was living with her spouse in 1935 in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States.3
  • Helen was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated at 7556 South Winchester Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, rents his house for $45, age 39, worked for 50 hours the previous week as a draft engineer at a packing house machine mfg, he worked for 52 weeks in 1939 and earned $2800.
    2. -----, Helen, age 38.
    3. -----, Helen A., age 15, attends school.
    4. -----, Paula E., age 13, attends school.
    5. -----, Frances, age 12, attends school.
    6. -----, Thomas, age 4, doesn't attend school.3
  • She died in February 1976, at age 74, in Story, Story County, Iowa, United States.1
