Emil Michael1

Parents

  • Father: Matt Michael (b. about 1866)
  • Mother: Augusta Miller (b. about 1868)
  • Emil Michael was born about 1903 in Nebraska, United States.1
  • He worked as a clerk for a meat packing company in 1930.1
  • Emil was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1517 Berry Ave were:
    1. Michael, Matt, owns his residence which was valued at $3000; age 64, works as a car inspector at a meat packing plant.
    2. -----, Augusta, age 62.
    3. -----, Walter, age 28, was an unemployed laborer in a meat packing plant.
    4. -----, Emil, age 26, worked as a clerk in a meat packing plant.
    5. -----, Frederick, age 23, worked as a machinist at a typewriter company.
    6. -----, Ida, age 18, doesn't attend school, no occupation.1,2
