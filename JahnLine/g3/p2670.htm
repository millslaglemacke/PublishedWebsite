Virginia Lelia Seybold1

Parents

  • Father: Louis J. Seybold (b. estimated 1890)
  • Mother: Lelia Smith (b. estimated 1890)
  • Virginia Lelia Seybold was born on 21 March 1911 in Turin, Monona County, Iowa, United States.1,2
  • She and Arthur John Bilek were married about 1928 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • She appeared on the 1930 census as an in-law to Frank Bartholomew Bilek in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3012 48th Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank B., rents dwelling for $30, works as a machinist for the railroad.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 48.
    3. -----, Arthur, age 22, works as a printing apprentice.
    4. -----, Virginia, age 19.2
  • She died on 5 January 1985, at age 73, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • She was buried estimated 8 January 1985 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
