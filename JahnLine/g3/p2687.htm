Hugh John ("Jack") O'Neill1

Parents

  • Father: Walter Francis O'Neill, Senior (b. 21 January 1900, d. May 1975)
  • Mother: Ann Marie Tourek (b. 8 February 1904, d. 3 February 1987)
  • Hugh John ("Jack") O'Neill was born on 14 April 1927 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • Hugh was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Living at 3927 F Street were:
    1. ONeill, Walter, rents his house for $30, age 30, he works as a salesman for a stockyard.
    2. -----, Ann, age 26.
    3. -----, Walter Jr., age 4 years 6 months.
    4. -----, Hugh, age 2 years 11 months.2
  • He and Alicia Ann Dooley were married on 1 May 1954 in Creston, Union County, Iowa, United States.1
  • He died on 3 November 1995, at age 68, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • He was buried estimated 6 November 1995 in Calvary Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
