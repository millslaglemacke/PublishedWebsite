Leonal Everett Barber1

Parents

  • Father: Travers Othelia Barber (b. about 1894)
  • Mother: Edna Wise (b. about 1893)
  • Leonal Everett Barber was also known as Bud Barber.1
  • He was born on 4 October 1913 in Washington, Daviess County, Indiana, United States.1,2
  • Leonal moved with his parents between April 1916 and September 1917 to Montana, United States.2
  • Leonal was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1920 Census in School District 111, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated at this house were:
    1. Barber, Travers O., head, rents dwelling, works on his own account on a general farm.
    2. -----, Edna, wife, age 26.
    3. -----, Lionel E., age 6, doesn't attend school.
    4. -----, John, age 3 8/12.
    5. -----, Robert T., age 2 3/12.
    6. -----, Gerald W., age 8/12.2
  • He and Emma Ruth Van Haur were married on 4 June 1940 in Great Falls, Cascade County, Montana, United States.1
  • He died on 31 August 1991, at age 77, in Denton, Fergus County, Montana, United States.1
  • He was buried after 31 August 1991 in Hillcrest Cemetery, Denton, MT, in Denton, Fergus County, Montana, United States.1
