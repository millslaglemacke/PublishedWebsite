Nancy Perdy1

Parents

  • Father: Top of the Line Perdy (b. estimated 1794)
  • Note.
  • Nancy Perdy was born on 22 October 1814 in Kentucky, United States.1,2
  • She has conflicting birth information of calculated 1821 in Virginia, United States.3
  • She and William Black were married on 24 February 1831 in Gallatin County, Illinois, United States.4
  • Nancy was enumerated on the 1850 census in the household of her spouse in Elizabethtown, Hardin County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Black, William, age 39, male, blacksmith.
    2. -----, Nancy, age 29, female.
    3. Blevins, Joseph, age 25, male, blacksmith.
    4. Black, Sarah Ann, age 17, female.
    5. -----, Martha J., age 15, female.
    6. -----, Rosamid, age 13, female, attends school.
    7. -----, John, age 11, male, attends school
    8. -----, Isaac, age 9, male, attends school.
    9. -----, Mary E., age 5, female.
    10. -----, William H., age 1, male.5
  • She appeared on the 1860 census as an in-law to Joseph N. Lee in Cave In Rock Post Office, Hardin County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Lee, Joseph N., age 31, farmer, $2500 real estate, $600 personal estate.
    2. -----. Martha J., age 25, housekeeper.
    3. -----, Wm N., age 8.
    4. -----, Sara J., age 6.
    5. -----. John V., age 5.
    6. Black, Nancy, age 40, mother.
    7. -----, John, age 20.
    8. -----, Isaac, age 17.
    9. -----, Mary, age 13.
    10. -----, Eliza P., age 10.6
  • She died on 21 March 1864, at age 49, in Hardin County, Illinois, United States.1
