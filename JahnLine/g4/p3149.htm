Ethel Irene Wariner1,2

Parents

  • Father: George Winfield Wariner (b. 21 August 1878, d. 9 June 1952)
  • Mother: Martha "Mattie" Rebecca ("Mattie") Smith (b. 17 April 1877, d. 20 September 1961)
  • Ethel Irene Wariner was born in 1902 in Texas, United States.1
  • She appeared on the 1910 census in the household of her parents in 2nd Judicial Precinct, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wossener, George Wossener, head, white, age 31, dentist, own shop, owns his own house free.
    2. -----, Martha, wife, age 31, has given birth to 3 children - all of them are living.
    3. -----, Ethel, age 7.
    4. -----, Berne, age 5
    5. -----, Ila, age 2.
    6. Downing, Balam Downing, age 53, works as a County Commissioner for a wage. [He is George's father-in-law]
    7. -----, Sarah J., age 54. [She is George's mother-in-law]1
  • She died in 1918, at age ~16.
