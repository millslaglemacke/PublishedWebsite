Mary Ann Pliska

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
  • Mary Ann Pliska was born in September 1879 in Bohemia.
  • Mary moved with her parents 23 May 1887.1
  • She and John Tourek were married on 3 July 1898 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She died on 31 May 1911, at age 31, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 3 June 1911 in St. Mary's Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
