Fredrick John Michael1

Parents

  • Father: Matt Michael (b. about 1866)
  • Mother: Augusta Miller (b. about 1868)
  • Fredrick John Michael was born on 1 September 1906 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • He worked as a machinist for a typewriter company in 1930.3,1
  • Fredrick was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1517 Berry Ave were:
    1. Michael, Matt, owns his residence which was valued at $3000; age 64, works as a car inspector at a meat packing plant.
    2. -----, Augusta, age 62.
    3. -----, Walter, age 28, was an unemployed laborer in a meat packing plant.
    4. -----, Emil, age 26, worked as a clerk in a meat packing plant.
    5. -----, Frederick, age 23, worked as a machinist at a typewriter company.
    6. -----, Ida, age 18, doesn't attend school, no occupation.3,1
  • He and Jeanne Emilie Jahn were married on 12 June 1934 in Nebraska, United States.
  • In 1935, Fredrick resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 16 April 1940 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5059 Star were:
    1. Michael, Frederic J., owns his house which was valued at 3000, age 33, worked 44 hours the previous week as in maintenance service department for a typewriter company.
    2. -----, Jean J., age 32, is employed in housework.
    3. -----, Marilyn J., age 3.1
  • He died on 22 August 1979, at age 72, in Lincoln, Lancaster County, Nebraska, United States.2
  • He was buried estimated 25 August 1979 in Lincoln Memorial Park, Lincoln, Lancaster County, Nebraska, United States.1
