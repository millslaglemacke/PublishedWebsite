Rose Frances Sadil1

Parents

  • Father: Stanley Frank Sadil (b. 2 January 1870, d. 23 February 1939)
  • Mother: Frances Mary Pliska (b. 10 June 1877, d. 16 July 1948)
  • Rose Frances Sadil was born on 12 March 1915 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • Rose was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5801 South 24th Street were:
    1. Sadil, Stanley F., owns his house which was valued at $3000, age 60, works as a retail merchant for a notions store, is not a vet.
    2. -----, Frances, age 55.
    3. -----, Agnes, age 17, works as a seamstress in an overall factory.
    4. -----, Rose, age 15, attends school.2
  • She and Donald Clyde Marlenee were married about 1935 in Papillion, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States, They later divorced.1
  • She and Michael Mitchell Rojewski were married on 30 April 1949 in Papillion, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • She died on 31 May 2003, at age 88, in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 3 June 2003 in National Memorial Cemetery, Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.
