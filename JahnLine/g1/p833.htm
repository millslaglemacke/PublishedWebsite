Charles Ernest Baltzer

  • Charles Ernest Baltzer was born on 15 July 1895 in Atlantc, Cass County, Iowa, United States.1,2
  • He served in the military the military during World War I in 1917.3
  • He and Mary Katherine Sadil were married on 1 November 1922 in Des Moines, Polk County, Iowa, United States.3,1
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 3 April 1930 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave, were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $3500,works as a clerk in a packing house, is a world war veteran.
    2. -----, Mary, age 24.
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 6, attends school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 3 years and 2 months.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 2 years and 1 month.1
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 3 April 1940 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $1000, age 44, completed 1 year of high school, he was born in Iowa. He was employed as a clerk in the meat packing industry. He worked 42 hours last week and worked 52 weeks last year earning $1,248. This family lived in the same house in 1935.
    2. -----, Mary, age 34, completed 8th grade
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 16, attends school, completed 2 years of high school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 13, attends school, completed 7th grade.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 12, attends school, completed 6th grade.
    6. -----, Diane, age 8, attends school, completed 2nd grade.
    7. -----, Robert, age 6, attends school, completed 1st grade.
    8. -----, Marilynn, age 4
    9. -----, Ronald, age 1.2
  • He died on 20 December 1973, at age 78, in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.3
  • He was buried estimated 23 December 1973 in Fort Whipple National Cemetery, Prescott, AZ, in Prescott, Yavapai County, Arizona, United States.3
