Gladys Shepherd1

Parents

  • Father: Shepherd (b. estimated 1869)
  • Mother: Addie (b. about 1869)
  • Gladys Shepherd was born on 21 January 1894 in Brownwood, Brown County, Texas, United States.1,2
  • She and Styles Martin Kennemer were married about 1924.3
  • She appeared on the 1930 census in the household of her mother in Wichita Falls, Wichita County, Texas, United States. Enumerated at 2203 9th St were:
    1. Shepherd, Addie, head, age 61, no occupation.
    2. -----, Charles H. ab, son, age 21, single, attends school. [NOTE: I think the ab written after his name means he was absent. He is also marked for attending school so I think maybe he was living at the school.]
    3. Kennemer, Stiles M., son-in-law, age 37, works as a salesman in radio.
    4. -----, Gladis, daughter, age 36, no occupation.
    5. -----, Donald K., grandson, age 2 8/12.3
  • She died on 9 September 1956, at age 62, in Wichita Falls, Wichita County, Texas, United States.1

Family: Styles Martin Kennemer (b. 8 October 1892, d. 19 December 1984)

  • Donald Keith Kennemer (b. about July 1927)
