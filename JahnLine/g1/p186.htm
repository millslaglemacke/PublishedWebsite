Emma Beatrice Pliska

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
  • Emma Beatrice Pliska was born on 4 August 1891 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4,5
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.6
  • She appeared on the 1910 census on 29 April 1910 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 929 N. 22nd Street were:
    1. Schindel, Dr. CM, age 47.
    2. -----, LaVilla W., wife, age 45.
    3. -----, Louise, daughter, age 19.
    4. -----, Crombie, son, age 16.
    5. Pliska, Emma B., servant, age 19, works as a housemaid in the home for wages.
    6. Waddell, Belle J., mother-in-law, age 68.2
  • She and Henry Van Haur were married on 11 November 1911 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.3
  • Emma moved with her spouse May 1917 to Montana, United States.7
  • Emma was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, owns his farm, age 35, he works as a farmer.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 28.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 7, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 5 years 5 months, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 2 years 10 months.3
  • Emma was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm, he works as a farmer on a general farm on his "own account."
    2. -----, Emma B., age 3.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 16, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 14, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 12, attends school.4
  • Emma was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm for $20, age 56.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 48.
    3. -----, Henrietta, age 22, completed 1 year of college, works as a typist for a real estate office.5
  • She died on 18 June 1963, at age 71, in Lewistown, Fergus County, Montana, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 21 June 1963 in Lewistown City Cemetery, Lewistown, Fergus County, Montana, United States.
