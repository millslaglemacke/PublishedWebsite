Grover Leonard ("Coach") Cannaday1

Parents

  • Father: Grover Cannaday (b. about 1893, d. before 1974)
  • Mother: Mary Ellen Skidmore (b. 21 September 1894, d. 12 May 1974)
  • Grover Leonard ("Coach") Cannaday was born on 2 October 1916.2,1
  • Grover was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Precinct 1, Franklin County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Cannaday, Grover, age 37, works as a general farmer on his own account.
    2. -----, Mary E., wife, age 35.
    3. -----, Grover L., son, age 13, attends school.
    4. -----, Charles E., son, age 10.
    5. -----, J. C., son, age 3 years and 2 months.3
  • He and Jerry ("Nell") Downing were married about July 1942.4
  • About March 1988, Grover resided in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.2
  • He died on 10 April 1988, at age 71, in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.2
  • He was buried estimated 13 April 1988 in Purley Baptist Church, Purley, Franklin County, Texas, United States.1
