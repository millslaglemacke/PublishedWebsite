Hattie Jane Johnson

Parents

  • Father: Alexander Johnson (b. 2 August 1816, d. 10 April 1888)

  • She was also known as Lee Kennemer.1
  • She was born on 1 July 1866 in Biardstown, Lamar County, Texas, United States.2,1,3,4,5
  • She and George Washington Kennemer were married on 19 December 1883 in Lamar County, Texas, United States.6,7,1,8
