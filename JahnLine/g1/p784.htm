Ann Marie Tourek

Parents

  • Father: John Tourek (b. January 1874, d. 19 October 1951)
  • Mother: Mary Ann Pliska (b. September 1879, d. 31 May 1911)
  • Ann Marie Tourek was born on 8 February 1904 in Verdigre, Knox County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • She and Walter Francis O'Neill, Senior, were married on 1 January 1925 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.2,1
  • Ann was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Living at 3927 F Street were:
    1. ONeill, Walter, rents his house for $30, age 30, he works as a salesman for a stockyard.
    2. -----, Ann, age 26.
    3. -----, Walter Jr., age 4 years 6 months.
    4. -----, Hugh, age 2 years 11 months.1
  • She died on 3 February 1987, at age 82, in Plattsmouth, Cass County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 6 February 1987 in Calvary Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
