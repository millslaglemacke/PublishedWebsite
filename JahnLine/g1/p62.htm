Sarah Jane Lee1

Parents

  • Father: Joseph N. Lee (b. 17 February 1827, d. 8 September 1862)
  • Mother: Martha J. Black (b. 29 September 1834, d. 19 August 1919)
  • Sarah Jane Lee was also known as Jane Smith.2
  • She was born in January 1854 in Illinois, United States.3,4,5,6
  • Sarah was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1860 Census in Cave In Rock Post Office, Hardin County, Illinois, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Lee, Joseph N., age 31, farmer, $2500 real estate, $600 personal estate.
    2. -----. Martha J., age 25, housekeeper.
    3. -----, Wm N., age 8.
    4. -----, Sara J., age 6.
    5. -----. John V., age 5.
    6. Black, Nancy, age 40, mother.
    7. -----, John, age 20.
    8. -----, Isaac, age 17.
    9. -----, Mary, age 13.
    10. -----, Eliza P., age 10.7
  • She and William Champion Smith were married in 1877 in Elizabethtown, Hardin County, Missouri, United States.8,4
  • Sarah was enumerated on the 1880 census in the household of her spouse in Caldwell County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this dwelling were:
    1. Sawier, J. A. J, white, 23, married, farming.
    2. -----, Marthy, white, 19, wife, married.
    3. Farmer, Mary, black, female, 35, married, cooking.
    4. McMakoon, W. J., white, male, 30, hirling, single, farming.
    5. Smith, WC, white, male, 40, married, farming, unemployed 11 months, consumption.
    6. -----, Jane, white, female, 26, wife.
    7. -----, Matty R., white, female, 2, daughter.4
  • She and Baalam Downing were married on 16 February 1881 in Hunt County, Texas, United States.9
  • Between 1900 and 1941, Sarah resided in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States.1
  • Sarah was enumerated on the 1900 census in the household of her spouse in Justice Precinct #1, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, B.M., born Jan 1854, age 46, farmer, can read and write, owns farm free..
    2. -----, Sarah J., born Jan 1854, age 46, has had 2 children, both still living.
    3.-----, Mattie, born Apr 1878, age 22, (She is listed as B.M.'s daughter and not step-daughter)
    4. -----, Oscar, born Nov 1881, age 18.8
  • George is Sarah's son-in-law.10
  • She appeared on the 1920 census as an in-law to George Winfield Wariner in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. George Wariner, age 41, born Missouri, parents born US, works as a pipe fitter.
    2. -----, Mattie, age 38, born TX, parents born TX.
    3. -----, Verne, son, age 18 (15?).
    4. -----, Ila Myrle, daughter, age 11.
    5. -----, BM, son, age 7.
    6. Downing, Sarah Jane, mother-in-law, 66, widow, born IL, father born IL, mother born VA.11
  • She appeared on the 1940 census as a mother to Martha "Mattie" Rebecca ("Mattie") Smith in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wariner, Mattie, rents house for $6, age 60, widow.
    2. Downing, Sarah, age 86, widow, unable to work.12
  • Sarah Jane Lee died on 16 February 1941, at age 87, in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States.1
  • She was buried on 17 February 1941 in Alvord, Wise County, Texas, United States.1
