Anna Madeline Pliska1

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
  • Anna Madeline Pliska was born on 2 January 1882 in Bohemia.2,3,4,5,1
  • Anna moved with her parents 23 May 1887.6
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.7
  • She and Frank Bartholomew Bilek were married on 4 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,5
  • Anna was enumerated on the 1900 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in at 223 N. 23rd Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 22, works as a laborer in a packing house, rents their house.
    2. -----, Annie, age 18, given birth to 0 children.8
  • The family moved to Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States, sometime between 1901 and 1906 but later moved back to Omaha.3
  • Anna was enumerated on the 1910 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3520 X Street were:
    1. Bielek, Frank, age 32, works as a laborer in a packing house, owns his house with a mortgage.
    2. -----, Annie, age 28.
    3. -----, Frank, age 9.
    4. -----, Rosie, age 6.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 3.3
  • Anna was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3128 S Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 43, works as a merchant.
    2. -----, Anna, age 39.
    3. -----, Frank, age 19, works as a sailor (or tailor).
    4. ----, Rosalind, age 16, attends school.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 12, attends school.4
  • Anna was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3012 48th Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank B., rents dwelling for $30, works as a machinist for the railroad.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 48.
    3. -----, Arthur, age 22, works as a printing apprentice.
    4. -----, Virginia, age 19.5
  • Anna was living with her spouse in 1935 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.9
  • Anna was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5024 Lake St. were:
    1. Balek, Frank B., owns his house which was valued at $5500, age 63, worked 40 hours last week as a mcahinist for the railroad; he earned $1340 in 1939.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 58.9
  • She died on 29 October 1951, at age 69, in Tecumseh, Johnson County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 1 November 1951 in Holy Sepulchre Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
