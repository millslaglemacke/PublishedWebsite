Arthur John Bilek1,2

Parents

  • Father: Frank Bartholomew Bilek (b. 11 January 1878, d. 31 October 1946)
  • Mother: Anna Madeline Pliska (b. 2 January 1882, d. 29 October 1951)
  • Arthur John Bilek was born on 12 April 1907 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4
  • He was christened on 17 April 1907 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.2
  • He appeared on the 1910 census in the household of his parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3520 X Street were:
    1. Bielek, Frank, age 32, works as a laborer in a packing house, owns his house with a mortgage.
    2. -----, Annie, age 28.
    3. -----, Frank, age 9.
    4. -----, Rosie, age 6.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 3.4
  • Arthur was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1920 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3128 S Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 43, works as a merchant.
    2. -----, Anna, age 39.
    3. -----, Frank, age 19, works as a sailor (or tailor).
    4. ----, Rosalind, age 16, attends school.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 12, attends school.1
  • He and Virginia Lelia Seybold were married about 1928 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.2,3
  • Arthur was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3012 48th Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank B., rents dwelling for $30, works as a machinist for the railroad.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 48.
    3. -----, Arthur, age 22, works as a printing apprentice.
    4. -----, Virginia, age 19.3
  • He died on 14 January 1996, at age 88, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • He was buried estimated 17 January 1996 in Calvary Cemetery, Omaha, NE, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
