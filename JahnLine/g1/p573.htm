Alice Octavia Frazier1

  • Alice Octavia Frazier was born on 2 September 1860 in Texas, United States.1,2,3
  • She and Obediah Carter Downing were married in 1878.1
  • Alice was enumerated on the 1880 census in the household of her spouse in District #2, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, OC, age 24, farmer.
    2. -----, AO, age 20.
    3. -----, Allie, age 1, born TX.
    4. -----, BM, age 26 (relationship to head - brother), farmer.
    5.-----, Travis B, age 8/12 (relationship to head - newphew).2
  • Alice was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Texline, Dallam County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Obediah, owns his house free, age 63, is a dry goods merchant that employs others.
    2. ----, Alice, age 59.
    3. -----, Jatta, age 31.3
  • She died on 8 July 1941, at age 80, in Texline, Dallam County, Texas, United States.1
