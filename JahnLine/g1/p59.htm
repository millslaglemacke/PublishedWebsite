Johann J. Pliska1

Parents

  • Father: Top of the Line Pliska
  • Mother: Aneska (b. 20 January 1820, d. 28 October 1895)
  • Johann J. Pliska was born in May 1852 in Bohemia.2
  • He and Francesa Dufek were married estimated 1875.
  • He immigrated to America on the ship Strassberg on 23 May 1887.3
  • He appeared on the 1900 census on 2 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.4
  • He died on 7 January 1910, at age 57, in South Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • He was buried estimated 9 January 1910 in St. Mary's Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.5
