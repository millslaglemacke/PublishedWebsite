William T. Downing, III

Parents

  • Father: William T. Downing, Junior (b. 4 September 1814, d. 29 January 1874)
  • Mother: Charity Brown (b. 9 January 1817, d. after 1880)
  • William T. Downing, III, was born about 1844 in Missouri, United States.
  • William was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1850 Census in Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household are:
    1. Downing, William, age 35, farmer, real estate valued at $400.
    2. -----, Charity, age 33.
    3. -----, Rachel, age 15, attends school (I believe this is Rhoda).
    4. -----, Rebecca, age 11, attends school.
    5. -----, Thos. B., age 9, attends school.
    6. -----, William T., age 6, attends school.
    7. -----, John B., age 4.
    8. -----, Mary C., age 3.
    Missing from the household is Joel (age 6). I suspect he and Willliam T. are the same person.1
  • He and Nancy were married about 1868.2
  • He appeared on the 1880 census on 11 June 1880 in Precinct 5, Travis County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, William, age 35, farmer.
    2. -----, Nancy, age 36.
    3. -----, Lena, age 11.
    4. -----, William, 8.
    5. -----, Ida, 4.
    6. -----, Josephine, 6/12 born Nov.
    7. Holmes, John, age 23, border, works on farm.2
