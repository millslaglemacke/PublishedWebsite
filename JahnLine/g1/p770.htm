Fred Ratay, Junior

Parents

  • Father: Fred James Ratay, Sr. (b. 14 October 1879)
  • Mother: Josephine ("Josie") Jahn (b. about 1880)
  • Fred Ratay, Junior, was born about 1903 in Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • Fred was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1910 Census in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, age 30, works as a laborer in a packing company for wages, owns his house "free."
    2. -----, Josie, age 30.
    3. -----, Mary, age 9, attends school.
    4. -----, Joseph, age 8, attends school.
    5. -----, Fred, age 7, attends school.
    6. -----, James, age 5.
    7. -----, Anna, age 3.2
  • Fred was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1920 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1334 Drexel Street were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, head, owns his house "free", age 40, works as a millwright for a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Josie, wife, age 40.
    3. -----, Mary E., daughter, age 19, works as a forelady for a cracker company.
    4. -----, Joseph, son, age 18, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    5. -----, Fred, son, age 16, attends school, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    6. -----, James, son, 15, attends school.
    7. -----, Anna, daughter, age 13, attends school.
    8.-----, Helen, daughter, age 9, attends school.
    9. -----, Edward, son 4 1/12.
    10. Jahn, Frank, brother in law, age 29, works as a janitor for a "cost office." [note: it transcribed as cost house but I suspect it may be post office.]1
  • He died in 1925.
