Lillian Emiline Sadil1

Parents

  • Father: Stanley Frank Sadil (b. 2 January 1870, d. 23 February 1939)
  • Mother: Frances Mary Pliska (b. 10 June 1877, d. 16 July 1948)
  • Lillian Emiline Sadil was born on 28 September 1909 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.2,3
  • She and Clarence Joseph Bartlett, Senior, were married on 10 May 1926 in Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa, United States.4
  • Lillian moved with her spouse between September 1929 and April 1930 to Denver, Denver County, Colorado, United States.2
  • Lillian moved with her spouse between 1930 and 1935 to Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.3
  • Lillian was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Denver, Denver County, Colorado, United States. Enumerated at 1338 W. Alameda Ave were:
    1. Bartlett, Clarence, rents his dwelling for $25, works as an assembler in an auto parts factory (currently employed).
    2. -----, Lillian E., age 20, first married at 16.
    3. -----, Clarence Jr., age 2 years 10 months.
    4. -----, Robert L., age 8 months.2
  • She died on 6 August 1981, at age 71, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 9 August 1981 in Calvary Cemetery, Omaha, NE, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
