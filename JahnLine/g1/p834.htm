Dorothy Mae Baltzer1

Parents

  • Father: Charles Ernest Baltzer (b. 15 July 1895, d. 20 December 1973)
  • Mother: Mary Katherine Sadil (b. 1 November 1905, d. 27 October 1995)
  • Dorothy Mae Baltzer was born on 6 October 1923 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3
  • Dorothy was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1930 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave, were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $3500,works as a clerk in a packing house, is a world war veteran.
    2. -----, Mary, age 24.
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 6, attends school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 3 years and 2 months.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 2 years and 1 month.2
  • She appeared on the 1940 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $1000, age 44, completed 1 year of high school, he was born in Iowa. He was employed as a clerk in the meat packing industry. He worked 42 hours last week and worked 52 weeks last year earning $1,248. This family lived in the same house in 1935.
    2. -----, Mary, age 34, completed 8th grade
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 16, attends school, completed 2 years of high school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 13, attends school, completed 7th grade.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 12, attends school, completed 6th grade.
    6. -----, Diane, age 8, attends school, completed 2nd grade.
    7. -----, Robert, age 6, attends school, completed 1st grade.
    8. -----, Marilynn, age 4
    9. -----, Ronald, age 1.3
  • She and Dan Jarvis Tilford were married on 9 January 1943 in Papillion, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States, They later divorced.4
  • She and Cecil Jackson Austin were married on 26 January 1955 in Globe, Gila County, Arizona, United States.4
  • She and Louis Ralph Frederick were married on 9 December 1980 in Paradise Valley, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.4
