Theodore Page Jestes1

Parents

  • Father: Forest Page Jestes (b. 23 October 1896, d. 12 July 1981)
  • Mother: Marue Elizabeth Page (b. 20 March 1902, d. 18 June 1992)
  • Theodore Page Jestes was born on 24 December 1923 in Arkansas City, Cowley County, Kansas, United States.1
  • He served in the military the Army Air Corps as a private on 21 January 1943 in Italy.1
  • He and Sharon Jean Eivar were married on 30 March 1951 They divorced on 26 May 1955.1
  • He and Diane Baltzer were married on 23 August 1957 in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.1
  • He was employed in 1965. He had many related jobs over his career. He worked in electrical distribution as a lineman, a troubleman, and a local representative at the Grand Canyon for 16 years. He was the area manager at Winslow, Arizona (included the Hopi Reservation) for 6 years. He managed offices in the Goodyear and Sun City area. He retired from the Arizona Public Service Company (electric company).1
  • He died on 9 November 1998, at age 74.
