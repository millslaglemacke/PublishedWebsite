Charity Brown1

Parents

  • Father: Thomas Brown (b. about 1785, d. 1828)
  • Mother: Lurana A. Hill (b. May 1787, d. 11 November 1851)
  • Charity Brown was born on 9 January 1817 in Maury County, Tennessee, United States.2,3,4,5
  • She and William T. Downing, Junior, were married 10 January 1833 or 30 January 1833 in Bond County, Illinois, United States.6,7
  • Charity was enumerated on the 1850 census in the household of her spouse in Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household are:
    1. Downing, William, age 35, farmer, real estate valued at $400.
    2. -----, Charity, age 33.
    3. -----, Rachel, age 15, attends school (I believe this is Rhoda).
    4. -----, Rebecca, age 11, attends school.
    5. -----, Thos. B., age 9, attends school.
    6. -----, William T., age 6, attends school.
    7. -----, John B., age 4.
    8. -----, Mary C., age 3.
    Missing from the household is Joel (age 6). I suspect he and Willliam T. are the same person.8
  • Charity was enumerated on the 1860 census in the household of her spouse in Greenfield Post Office, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Living in this house were:
    1. Downing, William, age 45, works as a farmer, real estate valued at $2000, personal estate valued at $850.
    2. -----, Charity, age 44.
    3. Knight, Rebecca, age 21 [She is William & Charity's widowed daughter.]
    4. Downing, Thomas, age 18, works as farm labor.
    5. -----, Joel, age 16.
    6. -----, John, age 14.
    7. -----, Elisabeth, age 12, attends school.
    8. -----, Baalam, age 6, attends school.
    9. -----, Obediah, age 3.
    10. Knight, William, age 2, born TX. [He is Rebecca's son and William and Charity's grandson.].
    Living in the next house enumerated is William's brother, Carter L. Downing. Missing from the household are William and Mary. I suspect that William and Joel are the same person based on their birthdates and the fact that neither one appear on the census records together.9
  • Charity was enumerated on the 1870 census in the household of her spouse in Precinct 4, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, William, age 55, farmer, real estate $1000, personal estate $630.
    2. -----, Charity, age 55.
    3. -----, Baalam, age 16.
    4. -----, Obediah, age 13.
    5. -----, William, age 11, attends school.
    6. Denton, Mary [daughter of Rhoda], age 16.
    7. Hobbs, Wm [not entered; unknown relationship], age 20.
    8. Denton, Florida, age 14, attends school.10
  • She died after 1880 in Hunt County, Texas, United States.1
