Agnes Alice Pliska

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
  • Agnes Alice Pliska was born on 27 March 1893 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.4
  • She appeared on the 1910 census on 16 April 1910 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 566 35th Ave were:
    1. Benedict, Dwight, age 47, born Pennsylvania, is president of a lumber firm
    2. -----, Oleanor, age 40, born New York
    3. -----, Ralph, age 19, born Nebrasksa
    4. Pliska, Agnes A., age 17, born Nebraska, her parents were born in Austria/Bohemia, she was unemployed 0 weeks in 1909, she does housework for a private family, she is able to read, write, and speak English.3
  • She and Frank Joseph Woksa, Senior, were married on 10 September 1913 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She died on 16 September 1974, at age 81, in Council Bluffs, Pottawattamie County, Iowa, United States.2
  • She was buried estimated 19 September 1974 in Plumer Settlement Cemetery, Mills County, Iowa, United States.
