Henrietta Van Haur1

Parents

  • Father: Henry Van Haur (b. 15 July 1884, d. 21 January 1964)
  • Mother: Emma Beatrice Pliska (b. 4 August 1891, d. 18 June 1963)
  • Henrietta Van Haur was born on 22 February 1917 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3
  • Henrietta moved with her parents May 1917 to Montana, United States.4
  • Henrietta was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1920 Census in Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, owns his farm, age 35, he works as a farmer.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 28.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 7, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 5 years 5 months, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 2 years 10 months.1
  • She was christened on 10 July 1927 in Hilger, Fergus County, Montana, United States.4
  • Henrietta was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1930 Census in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm, he works as a farmer on a general farm on his "own account."
    2. -----, Emma B., age 3.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 16, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 14, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 12, attends school.2
  • She was educated at a business school about 1935.4
  • She appeared on the 1940 census in the household of her parents in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm for $20, age 56.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 48.
    3. -----, Henrietta, age 22, completed 1 year of college, works as a typist for a real estate office.3
  • She and Floyd Harlow ("Buster") Ellis were married on 22 October 1941 in Lewistown, Fergus County, Montana, United States.4
