Anna Jahn

Parents

  • Father: Adam M. Jahn (b. about 1853, d. 22 June 1907)
  • Mother: Caterina Maurek (b. about 1853, d. after 1900)
  • Anna Jahn was born about 1896.1
  • She immigrated with her parents from Bremen on 5 Aug 1900 onboard the Koenigin Luise and arrived at Ellis Island, New York, United States, on 15 August 1900.2
