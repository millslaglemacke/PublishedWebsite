Jerry ("Nell") Downing1

Parents

  • Father: Thomas ("Oscar") Downing (b. 27 November 1881, d. 11 February 1949)
  • Mother: Molly Verona Kennemer (b. 18 June 1887, d. 19 February 1974)
  • Jerry ("Nell") Downing was also known as Nellie Downing.2
  • She was born on 27 September 1915 in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States.1,3,4
  • Jerry was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1920 Census in Byers, Clay County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Thomas O., rented home, age 38, worked a a laborer in oil refining for a wage.
    2. -----, Mollie B., age 32.
    3. -----, Gertrude, age 12, attended school.
    4. -----, Nell, age 4 years 8 months.5
  • Jerry was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1930 Census in Justice Precinct #2, Wichita County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, Oscar; renting at $10 monthly, owns radio, age 48, worked in oil fields, not a veteran.
    2. -----, Mollie; age 42.
    3. -----, Nellie; age 14, attending school.
    4. -----, Billie J.; age 8, attending school.2
  • Jerry was living in Union County, New Mexico, United States, on 1 April 1935.6
  • She and Kitts were married before June 1937 They later divorced.4,7
  • She appeared on the 1940 census in the household of her parents in Montague County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. -----, Oscar, age 58, he worked as a farmer for 50 weeks on his own account, he received more than $50 doing other work.
    2. -----, Mollie, age 52. .
    3. -----, Nell, age 23, marital status is divorced
    4. -----, Billie Jane, age 18, attending high school.6
  • She and Grover Leonard ("Coach") Cannaday were married about July 1942.4
  • She died on 21 January 2005, at age 89.4
  • She was buried estimated 24 January 2005 in Purley Baptist Church, Purley, Franklin County, Texas, United States.8
