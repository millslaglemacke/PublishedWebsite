Maxine Busing Jahn
  • Maxine Dean Busing was born on 8 November 1915 in Nebraska, United States.2,1
  • She and James Frank Jahn were married in 1934 in Kansas, United States.1
  • Maxine was living with her spouse in 1939 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. He was working as a city policeman.3
  • Maxine was living with her spouse in 1942 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.4
  • Maxine was living with her spouse in 1948 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. He was working as a salesman.5
  • In 1993, Maxine resided at Boulder, Boulder County, Colorado, United States.6
  • In May 2000, Maxine resided in Aurora, Arapahoe County, Colorado, United States.7
  • She died on 11 May 2000, at age 84, in Aurora, Arapahoe County, Colorado, United States.7
