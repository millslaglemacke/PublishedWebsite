Adam M. Jahn1

Parents

  • Father: Johann Jahn, von Neumark (b. estimated 1833)
  • Mother: Katherine (b. estimated 1833)
  • Adam M. Jahn was also known as Adam Jahn, von Neumark.
  • He was born about 1853 in Bohemia.2
  • He and Caterina Maurek were married estimated 1878.
  • He immigrated on 15 August 1900 to Ellis Island, New York, United States. They sailed from Bremen, Germany on 5 Aug 1900 on the Koengin Luise and landed in New York on 15 Aug 1900.
    1. -----, Adam, age 47, farmer, Omaha Neb, had $140, 1st time to US, has a daughter in Neb [is this Josephine?], from Austria, Province-Bohemia, mother tongue-Bohemian, Austrian subjects.
    2. -----, Katerina, age 47
    3. -----, Vaclav, age 16, laborer
    4. -----, Frank, age 10
    5. -----, Anna, age 4.3
  • He died on 22 June 1907.
