Josephine ("Josie") Jahn

Parents

  • Father: Adam M. Jahn (b. about 1853, d. 22 June 1907)
  • Mother: Caterina Maurek (b. about 1853, d. after 1900)
  • Josephine ("Josie") Jahn was born about 1880 in Bohemia.1,2
  • She immigrated in 1889 to Nebraska, United States. reportedly immigrated to the US with a wealthy family in 1889 when she was only 9 years old. Her family followed in 1900. On her father's passenger manifest, he lists that he has a daughter already living in Nebraska.3,1,2
  • She and Fred James Ratay, Sr., were married about 1900.3,1,2
  • Josephine was enumerated on the 1910 census in the household of her spouse in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, age 30, works as a laborer in a packing company for wages, owns his house "free."
    2. -----, Josie, age 30.
    3. -----, Mary, age 9, attends school.
    4. -----, Joseph, age 8, attends school.
    5. -----, Fred, age 7, attends school.
    6. -----, James, age 5.
    7. -----, Anna, age 3.2
  • Josephine was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1334 Drexel Street were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, head, owns his house "free", age 40, works as a millwright for a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Josie, wife, age 40.
    3. -----, Mary E., daughter, age 19, works as a forelady for a cracker company.
    4. -----, Joseph, son, age 18, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    5. -----, Fred, son, age 16, attends school, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    6. -----, James, son, 15, attends school.
    7. -----, Anna, daughter, age 13, attends school.
    8.-----, Helen, daughter, age 9, attends school.
    9. -----, Edward, son 4 1/12.
    10. Jahn, Frank, brother in law, age 29, works as a janitor for a "cost office." [note: it transcribed as cost house but I suspect it may be post office.].3
  • Josephine was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Living in this household were:
    1. Ratay, Fred J., owns his house which was valued at $7000, age 50, works as a carpenter in the stock yards.
    2. -----, Josephine, age 50.
    3. -----, Joe F.,age 28, works as a laborer on a fruit farm.
    4. -----, Helen, age 19, works as a musician on the concert stage.
    5. -----, Edward, age 14, attends school.
    6. ----, Alice, age 7, attends school.1
