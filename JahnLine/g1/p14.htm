Edward Jahn
  • Edward Louis Johann Jahn was born on 11 March 1915 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4
  • He appeared on the 1920 census in the household of his parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 4609 20th Street South were:
    1. Jahn, James, age 35, married, immigrated in 1900, naturalized in 1917, owns a bakery.
    2. -----, Antoinette, age 33, married, unknown when immigrated, naturalized (no date given).
    3. -----, Jeanne, age 12, attends school.
    4. -----, James, age 9, attends school.
    5. -----, Edward, age 4, attends school.5
  • Edward was listed as a child in the household of his parents on the 1930 census. He is aged 15 and attends school.6
  • He served in the military as a Midshipman, third class while at the U.S. Naval Academy. on 9 June 1932 in Anapolis, Anne Arundel County, Maryland, United States.7
  • He served in the military the army between June 1933 and April 1948.8,2
  • Edward was living at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States, in 1935.9
  • In 1936, Edward resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. He is living with his parents and his occupation is "student."
  • Edward was living in the household of his parents at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States, in 1936. Two of their children, James F. and Edward L,. also live at this residence.10
  • In 1942, Edward resided in Camp Hood, Bell County, Texas, United States.11
  • He was married on 6 December 1942 in Frederick, Tillman County, Oklahoma, United States, This couple planned to renew their vows on their 50th wedding anniversay but Billie Jane was ill around that time so they renewed their vows after 51 years. They filed for divorce in Oct 1993 so they could get remarried on Dec 6 1993.11,12,13
  • In November 1998, Edward resided in Temple, Bell County, Texas, United States.14
  • He died on 9 November 1998, at age 83, in Temple, Bell County, Texas, United States.15,14
