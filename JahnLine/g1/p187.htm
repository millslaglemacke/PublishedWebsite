Frank Bartholomew Bilek1

Parents

  • Father: Frank Bilek (b. estimated 1860)
  • Mother: Anna Wolf (b. estimated 1860)
  • Frank Bartholomew Bilek was buried esp 3 Nov 1946 in Holy Sepulchre Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • He was born on 11 January 1878 in Bohemia.1,2,3,4
  • He immigrated in 1882 to United States. He became a naturalized citizen in 1890.3,4,2
  • He and Anna Madeline Pliska were married on 4 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,4
  • He appeared on the 1900 census on 14 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in at 223 N. 23rd Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 22, works as a laborer in a packing house, rents their house.
    2. -----, Annie, age 18, given birth to 0 children.5
  • The family moved to Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States, sometime between 1901 and 1906 but later moved back to Omaha.2
  • He appeared on the 1910 census on 26 April 1910 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3520 X Street were:
    1. Bielek, Frank, age 32, works as a laborer in a packing house, owns his house with a mortgage.
    2. -----, Annie, age 28.
    3. -----, Frank, age 9.
    4. -----, Rosie, age 6.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 3.2
  • He appeared on the 1920 census on 12 January 1920 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3128 S Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 43, works as a merchant.
    2. -----, Anna, age 39.
    3. -----, Frank, age 19, works as a sailor (or tailor).
    4. ----, Rosalind, age 16, attends school.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 12, attends school.3
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 6 April 1930 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3012 48th Avenue were:
    1. Bilek, Frank B., rents dwelling for $30, works as a machinist for the railroad.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 48.
    3. -----, Arthur, age 22, works as a printing apprentice.
    4. -----, Virginia, age 19.4
  • In 1935, Frank resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.6
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 13 April 1940 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5024 Lake St. were:
    1. Balek, Frank B., owns his house which was valued at $5500, age 63, worked 40 hours last week as a mcahinist for the railroad; he earned $1340 in 1939.
    2. -----, Anna M., age 58.6
  • He died on 31 October 1946, at age 68, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
