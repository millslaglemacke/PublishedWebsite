Antoinette Celicia Pliska

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
Antoinette Pliska Jahn on her wedding day
  • Antoinette Celicia Pliska was also known as Antoinia Pliska.1
  • (Medical):See attached sources.2
  • She was born on 3 July 1886 in Brno, Moravia, Austria.3,4,2
  • She has conflicting birth information of 1887 in Nebraska, United States.5,6,7
  • Antoinette moved with her parents 23 May 1887.1
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.8
  • She and James John Jahn were married on 9 October 1904 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.9,10,11
  • Antoinette was enumerated on the 1910 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 478 22nd Street were:
    1. Jahn, James, age 26, married for 6 years, born Bohemia, immigrated in 1900, naturalized citizen, speaks English, baker.
    2. -----, Antoinette, age 23, married for 6 years, given birth to 2 children, born Bohemia, speaks English. [NOTE: I would expect this to say 3 children born and 2 living].
    3. -----, Jenny, age 2
    4. -----, Jimmy, age 4 or 9 months [the record is hard to decipher].12
  • Antoinette was enumerated on the 1920 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 4609 20th Street South were:
    1. Jahn, James, age 35, married, immigrated in 1900, naturalized in 1917, owns a bakery.
    2. -----, Antoinette, age 33, married, unknown when immigrated, naturalized (no date given).
    3. -----, Jeanne, age 12, attends school.
    4. -----, James, age 9, attends school.
    5. -----, Edward, age 4, attends school.13
  • Antoinette was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 4609 S. 20th Street:
    1. Jahn, James J., age 45, married, He owns his house which was valued at $2599 and owns a radio. He became a naturalized citizen in 1901. He owns a bakery and is not a veteran.
    2. -----, Antoinette, age 43, married, works at the bakery.
    3. -----, Jean E, age 22, works as a teacher in a public school.
    4. -----, Edward L., age 15, attends school.9
  • Antoinette was living with her spouse in 1935 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.14
  • Antoinette was living with her spouse in 1936 at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Two of their children, James F. and Edward L,. also live at this residence.15
  • Antoinette was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 4609 S. 20th Street:
    1. Jahn, James, age 55, married, highest grade attended was 8th grade, unable to work, received income of $50 or more from sources other than money wages or salary.
    2. -----, Antoinette, age 52, highest grade attended was 8th grade.16
  • In 1942, Antoinette resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.17
  • In 1948, Antoinette resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Her daughter-in-law, Billie Jane, was also listed as living in this residence but Ed (Billie Jane's husband) wasn't listed in this residence. It is also believed that Antoinette's granddaughter, Antoinette Rae Jahn, aged about 4 years, was living here as well.18
  • In June 1974, Antoinette resided in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.19,2
  • She died on 16 July 1974, at age 88, at Mission Manor Nursing Home, 501 Yates Street, Mount Vernon, TX, in Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.19,2
  • She was buried estimated 18 July 1974 in Mount Vernon Cemetery, Mount Vernon, Franklin County, Texas, United States.2,20
