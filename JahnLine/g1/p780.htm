Agnes Lucy Sadil1

Parents

  • Father: Stanley Frank Sadil (b. 2 January 1870, d. 23 February 1939)
  • Mother: Frances Mary Pliska (b. 10 June 1877, d. 16 July 1948)
  • Agnes Lucy Sadil was also known as Sister Mary Carmelita.2
  • She was born on 28 September 1909.1
  • She worked as a a nun that belonged to the Order of the Benedictine Nuns between 1930 and 1980 in Clyde, Nodaway County, Missouri, United States. She later moved to Benet Lake, Kenosha County, Wisconsin, United States. Her name was Sister Mary Carmelia. She later left the convent and lived in Phoenix for the last 20 or so years of her life.2
  • She appeared on the 1930 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5801 South 24th Street were:
    1. Sadil, Stanley F., owns his house which was valued at $3000, age 60, works as a retail merchant for a notions store, is not a vet.
    2. -----, Frances, age 55.
    3. -----, Agnes, age 17, works as a seamstress in an overall factory.
    4. -----, Rose, age 15, attends school.1
  • She died on 17 June 2000, at age 90, in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 19 June 2000 in St. Francis Catholic Cemetery, Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.
  • Last Edited: 10 July 2019
