Mary Katherine Sadil

Parents

  • Father: Stanley Frank Sadil (b. 2 January 1870, d. 23 February 1939)
  • Mother: Frances Mary Pliska (b. 10 June 1877, d. 16 July 1948)
  • Mary Katherine Sadil was also known as Mary Antonette Sadil.
  • She was born on 1 November 1905 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1,2
  • She and Charles Ernest Baltzer were married on 1 November 1922 in Des Moines, Polk County, Iowa, United States.3,1
  • Mary was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave, were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $3500,works as a clerk in a packing house, is a world war veteran.
    2. -----, Mary, age 24.
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 6, attends school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 3 years and 2 months.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 2 years and 1 month.1
  • Mary was enumerated on the 1940 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3524 S. 20th Ave were:
    1. Baltzer, Charles E., owns his house which was valued at $1000, age 44, completed 1 year of high school, he was born in Iowa. He was employed as a clerk in the meat packing industry. He worked 42 hours last week and worked 52 weeks last year earning $1,248. This family lived in the same house in 1935.
    2. -----, Mary, age 34, completed 8th grade
    3. -----, Dorothy M., age 16, attends school, completed 2 years of high school.
    4. -----, Myrtis M., age 13, attends school, completed 7th grade.
    5. -----, Charles S., age 12, attends school, completed 6th grade.
    6. -----, Diane, age 8, attends school, completed 2nd grade.
    7. -----, Robert, age 6, attends school, completed 1st grade.
    8. -----, Marilynn, age 4
    9. -----, Ronald, age 1.2
  • She died on 27 October 1995, at age 89, in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 30 October 1995 in Fort Whipple National Cemetery, Prescott, AZ, in Prescott, Yavapai County, Arizona, United States.
