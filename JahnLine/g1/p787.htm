Rosalind "Roz" Ann ("Roz") Bilek1

Parents

  • Father: Frank Bartholomew Bilek (b. 11 January 1878, d. 31 October 1946)
  • Mother: Anna Madeline Pliska (b. 2 January 1882, d. 29 October 1951)
  • Rosalind "Roz" Ann ("Roz") Bilek was born on 10 July 1903 in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois, United States.1,2
  • She appeared on the 1910 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3520 X Street were:
    1. Bielek, Frank, age 32, works as a laborer in a packing house, owns his house with a mortgage.
    2. -----, Annie, age 28.
    3. -----, Frank, age 9.
    4. -----, Rosie, age 6.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 3.2
  • Rosalind was enumerated in the household of her parents on the 1920 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 3128 S Street were:
    1. Bilek, Frank, age 43, works as a merchant.
    2. -----, Anna, age 39.
    3. -----, Frank, age 19, works as a sailor (or tailor).
    4. ----, Rosalind, age 16, attends school.
    5. -----, Arthur, age 12, attends school.1
  • She and Dr. Joseph Albert Lanspa were married on 30 May 1929 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.3
  • She died on 16 September 2000, at age 97, in Roseburg, Douglas County, Oregon, United States.
  • She was buried estimated 19 September 2000 in St. Joseph Cemetery, Roseburg, Douglas County, Oregon, United States.
