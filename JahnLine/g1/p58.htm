Caterina Maurek

Parents

  • Father: Top of the Line Maurek
  • Caterina Maurek was also known as Katherine Mourek.1
  • She was born about 1853 in Bohemia.2,3
  • She and Adam M. Jahn were married estimated 1878.
  • She and her husband immigrated with their children from Bremen on 5 Aug 1900 and arrived at Ellis Island, New York, United States, on 15 August 1900. The sailed on the ship Koenigin Luise.4
  • She died after 1900.

Family: Adam M. Jahn (b. about 1853, d. 22 June 1907)
