Henry Van Haur1

Parents

  • Father: Frank Van Haur (b. estimated 1860)
  • Mother: Aloise Grund (b. estimated 1860)
  • Henry Van Haur was born on 15 July 1884 in Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4
  • He and Emma Beatrice Pliska were married on 11 November 1911 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.1
  • Henry moved to Montana, United States, May 1917.4
  • He appeared on the 1920 census on 20 January 1920 in Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, owns his farm, age 35, he works as a farmer.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 28.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 7, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 5 years 5 months, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 2 years 10 months.1
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 4 April 1930 in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm, he works as a farmer on a general farm on his "own account."
    2. -----, Emma B., age 3.
    3. -----, Louise D., age 16, attends school.
    4. -----, Emma R., age 14, attends school.
    5. -----, Henrietta, age 12, attends school.2
  • He appeared on the 1940 census on 8 May 1940 in Plum Creek, Fergus County, Montana, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Van Haur, Henry, rents his farm for $20, age 56.
    2. -----, Emma B., age 48.
    3. -----, Henrietta, age 22, completed 1 year of college, works as a typist for a real estate office.3
  • He died on 21 January 1964, at age 79, in Great Falls, Cascade County, Montana, United States.
  • He was buried estimated 24 January 1964 in Lewistown City Cemetery, Lewistown, Fergus County, Montana, United States.
