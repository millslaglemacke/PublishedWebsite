Aneska

  • Aneska was also known as Aggie Jahn.1
  • She was also known as Agnes Jahn.1
  • She was born on 20 January 1820 in Moravia.
  • She and Top of the Line Pliska were married estimated 1850.
  • She immigrated to America with her son, Johann, and his family to America on the ship Strassburg 23 May 1887.1
  • She died on 28 October 1895, at age 75, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • She was buried on 29 October 1895 in St. Mary's Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.2
  • Her obituary was published in the the Omaha World-Herald on 29 October 1895 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. "PLISKA-Mrs. Aggie, age 75 years.
    Funeral today at 9:30 a.m., from family residence, 710 South , Seventh street to St. Wenceslaus church, Interment St. Mary's cemetery.".2
