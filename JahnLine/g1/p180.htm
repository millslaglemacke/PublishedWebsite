Frances Mary Pliska

Parents

  • Father: Johann J. Pliska (b. May 1852, d. 7 January 1910)
  • Mother: Francesa Dufek (b. about 1855, d. 26 December 1894)
  • Frances Mary Pliska was born on 10 June 1877 in Bohemia.1,2
  • Frances moved with her parents 23 May 1887.3
  • She appeared on the 1900 census in the household of her parents in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 2530 7th Ave, Omaha, Douglas Co., NE were:
    1. Pliska, John, head, age 48, widowed, works as a canned beef packer, unemployed 3 years, owns his home free.
    2. -----, Francis, daughter, age 23.
    3. -----, Anna, daughter, age 18, works as a shirt maker.
    4.-----, Tony, daughter, age 13, attended school for 9 months this year.
    5. -----, Emma, daughter, age 9.
    6. -----, Agnes, daughter, age 7.4
  • She and Stanley Frank Sadil were married on 4 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States, Stanley was 29 and Frances was 24 when they married.2
  • Frances was enumerated on the 1930 census in the household of her spouse in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5801 South 24th Street were:
    1. Sadil, Stanley F., owns his house which was valued at $3000, age 60, works as a retail merchant for a notions store, is not a vet.
    2. -----, Frances, age 55.
    3. -----, Agnes, age 17, works as a seamstress in an overall factory.
    4. -----, Rose, age 15, attends school.2
  • She died on 16 July 1948, at age 71, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
