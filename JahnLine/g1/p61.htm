Baalam Downing1

Parents

  • Father: William T. Downing, Junior (b. 4 September 1814, d. 29 January 1874)
  • Mother: Charity Brown (b. 9 January 1817, d. after 1880)
  • Baalam Downing was born on 6 January 1854 in Texas, United States.1,2,3,4
  • He appeared on the 1860 census in the household of his parents in Greenfield Post Office, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Living in this house were:
    1. Downing, William, age 45, works as a farmer, real estate valued at $2000, personal estate valued at $850.
    2. -----, Charity, age 44.
    3. Knight, Rebecca, age 21 [She is William & Charity's widowed daughter.]
    4. Downing, Thomas, age 18, works as farm labor.
    5. -----, Joel, age 16.
    6. -----, John, age 14.
    7. -----, Elisabeth, age 12, attends school.
    8. -----, Baalam, age 6, attends school.
    9. -----, Obediah, age 3.
    10. Knight, William, age 2, born TX. [He is Rebecca's son and William and Charity's grandson.].
    Living in the next house enumerated is William's brother, Carter L. Downing. Missing from the household are William and Mary. I suspect that William and Joel are the same person based on their birthdates and the fact that neither one appear on the census records together.1
  • He appeared on the 1870 census in the household of his parents in Precinct 4, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, William, age 55, farmer, real estate $1000, personal estate $630.
    2. -----, Charity, age 55.
    3. -----, Baalam, age 16.
    4. -----, Obediah, age 13.
    5. -----, William, age 11, attends school.
    6. Denton, Mary [daughter of Rhoda], age 16.
    7. Hobbs, Wm [not entered; unknown relationship], age 20.
    8. Denton, Florida, age 14, attends school.2
  • He and Susan Mills were married before 1879.
  • Baalam was listed as a brother on the 1880 census of Obediah Carter Downing enumerated on 24 June 1880 in District #2, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, OC, age 24, farmer.
    2. -----, AO, age 20.
    3. -----, Allie, age 1, born TX.
    4. -----, BM, age 26 (relationship to head - brother), farmer.
    5.-----, Travis B, age 8/12 (relationship to head - newphew).3
  • Baalam Downing and Sarah Jane Lee were married on 16 February 1881 in Hunt County, Texas, United States.5
  • He appeared on the 1900 census on 14 June 1900 in Justice Precinct #1, Hunt County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Downing, B.M., born Jan 1854, age 46, farmer, can read and write, owns farm free..
    2. -----, Sarah J., born Jan 1854, age 46, has had 2 children, both still living.
    3.-----, Mattie, born Apr 1878, age 22, (She is listed as B.M.'s daughter and not step-daughter)
    4. -----, Oscar, born Nov 1881, age 18.4
  • He appeared on the 1910 census as an in-law to George Winfield Wariner in 2nd Judicial Precinct, Wise County, Texas, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Wossener, George Wossener, head, white, age 31, dentist, own shop, owns his own house free.
    2. -----, Martha, wife, age 31, has given birth to 3 children - all of them are living.
    3. -----, Ethel, age 7.
    4. -----, Berne, age 5
    5. -----, Ila, age 2.
    6. Downing, Balam Downing, age 53, works as a County Commissioner for a wage. [He is George's father-in-law]
    7. -----, Sarah J., age 54. [She is George's mother-in-law]6
  • Baalam Downing died on 19 March 1916, at age 62, in Wise County, Texas, United States.
