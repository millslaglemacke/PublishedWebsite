Antoinette Rae Jahn1

Parents

  • Father: Edward Louis Johann Jahn (b. 11 March 1915, d. 9 November 1998)
Terri Jahn
  • Antoinette Rae Jahn was also known as Terri An Jahn.
  • She was also known as Toni Jahn.
  • She was also known as Terri Millslagle.
  • She was also known as Terri An Millslagle.
  • She was born on 30 March 1944 in Temple, Bell County, Texas, United States.1,2,3
  • In 1948, Antoinette resided at Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. She is believed to have been living with her mother and paternal grandmother. Her father was not listed as living in the household in this city directory.4
  • She was educated at All Saints Hospital in 1963 in Fort Worth, Tarrant County, Texas, United States.5
  • She was married on 15 August 1964 in Texas, United States.6
  • She died on 28 April 2014, at age 70, in Aguana, Riverside County, California, United States.
  • She was buried on 12 May 2014 in Riverside National Cemetery in Riverside, Riverside County, California, United States.
