Joseph Ratay

Parents

  • Father: Fred James Ratay, Sr. (b. 14 October 1879)
  • Mother: Josephine ("Josie") Jahn (b. about 1880)
  • Joseph Ratay was born on 23 March 1902 in Nebraska, United States.1,2,3,4,5,6
  • Joseph was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1910 Census in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated in this household were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, age 30, works as a laborer in a packing company for wages, owns his house "free."
    2. -----, Josie, age 30.
    3. -----, Mary, age 9, attends school.
    4. -----, Joseph, age 8, attends school.
    5. -----, Fred, age 7, attends school.
    6. -----, James, age 5.
    7. -----, Anna, age 3.2
  • Joseph was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1920 Census in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 1334 Drexel Street were:
    1. Ratay, Fred, head, owns his house "free", age 40, works as a millwright for a packing house for wages.
    2. -----, Josie, wife, age 40.
    3. -----, Mary E., daughter, age 19, works as a forelady for a cracker company.
    4. -----, Joseph, son, age 18, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    5. -----, Fred, son, age 16, attends school, works as an office clerk for a packing house.
    6. -----, James, son, 15, attends school.
    7. -----, Anna, daughter, age 13, attends school.
    8.-----, Helen, daughter, age 9, attends school.
    9. -----, Edward, son 4 1/12.
    10. Jahn, Frank, brother in law, age 29, works as a janitor for a "cost office." [note: it transcribed as cost house but I suspect it may be post office.]3
  • Joseph was enumerated in the household of his parents on the 1930 Census in Highland, Sarpy County, Nebraska, United States. Living in this household were:
    1. Ratay, Fred J., owns his house which was valued at $7000, age 50, works as a carpenter in the stock yards.
    2. -----, Josephine, age 50.
    3. -----, Joe F.,age 28, works as a laborer on a fruit farm.
    4. -----, Helen, age 19, works as a musician on the concert stage.
    5. -----, Edward, age 14, attends school.
    6. ----, Alice, age 7, attends school.4
  • He served in the military the U.S. Army during World War II between 15 October 1942 and 15 October 1943 in Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California, United States.1,7
  • Before September 1976, Joseph resided in Grand Island, Hall County, Nebraska, United States.5
  • He died on 13 September 1976, at age 74.8,6
  • He was buried estimated 16 September 1976 in Graceland Cemetery, South Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.6
