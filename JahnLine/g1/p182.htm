Stanley Frank Sadil1

  • Stanley Frank Sadil was born on 2 January 1870 in Prague, Bohemia.1
  • He immigrated in 1884 to United States. He became a natualized citizen sometime before the 1930 census.1
  • He and Frances Mary Pliska were married on 4 June 1900 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States, Stanley was 29 and Frances was 24 when they married.1
  • He appeared on the 1930 census on 9 April 1930 in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States. Enumerated at 5801 South 24th Street were:
    1. Sadil, Stanley F., owns his house which was valued at $3000, age 60, works as a retail merchant for a notions store, is not a vet.
    2. -----, Frances, age 55.
    3. -----, Agnes, age 17, works as a seamstress in an overall factory.
    4. -----, Rose, age 15, attends school.1
  • He died on 23 February 1939, at age 69, in Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
  • He was buried estimated 26 February 1939 in St. Mary's Cemetery, Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska, United States.
