Mother's maternal side:
  • Jahn - This volume contains my Jahn ancestry. They came through Ellis Island from Bohemia around the turn of the 20th century.
  • Downing - Focuses on the children of William and Charity (Brown) Downing. They married in Illinois, spent some time in Missouri, and then settled in Texas. It also includes the Kennemer family from Kennemer Cove, Alabama.
